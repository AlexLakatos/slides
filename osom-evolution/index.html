What's the catch?

WebFWD helps open source innovators

What are they offering?

  • Three-month curriculum to transform your project into a sustainable business
    • Weekly training sessions & exercises
    • Office hours with industry experts across various business disciplines
    • Ongoing coaching and guidance from the world's best technologists
  • Opportunity for funding from Mozilla upon completion of the program
  • Worldwide reach: The teams span the globe, participating wherever they are (examples: UK, France, India, Germany, Romania)
  • Next class: Every 3 months!
