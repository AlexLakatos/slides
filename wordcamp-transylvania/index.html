Ongoing work…

Mozilla is much more than Firefox though. We are a not-for-profit organisation to promote the open web. This means we educate people on how to publish content and we protect people's privacy and identity and choice on the web.

Webdev Team

We are Web developers who are responsible for the development of most of Mozilla's web sites, including addons.mozilla.org, mozilla.org, support.mozilla.org, Mozilla Developer Network, etc. All in all, Mozilla has well over 100 websites, from small internal tools over low-traffic campaigns with "only" a few thousand visitors a day to our largest applications serving several hundred million visitors a month, along with billions of API calls. Almost all of our websites are localizable, and some of them are translated into over 30 languages. All of our code is open source (much of it is on github), and patches are always welcome!
